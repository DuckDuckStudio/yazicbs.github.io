A Helper at Home

  Doing housework is very important for us. Why? Because doing housework not only can help family share housework, and also can make yourself more independent.

  Since housework is so important for us, what housework can we do? I think we can help family wash closes, cook, sweep the floor, take out the rubbish and so on. Among them, I most often help my family wash clothes.

  What did I learned while doing housework? I learned some skills in washing clothes. Is that all I learned? No. I also learned how to live on my own, after all you can't depend on your family all your life.

  Anyway, doing housework is very important for us, we not only can help family and also can learned some skills.

  Do some housework for your family now!

机翻

做家务对我们来说非常重要。为什么？因为做家务不仅可以帮助家人分担家务，还可以让自己更加独立。
既然家务活对我们来说如此重要，我们能做些什么家务呢？我认为我们可以帮助家人洗漱、做饭、扫地、倒垃圾等等。其中，我最常帮家人洗衣服。
我在做家务时学到了什么？我学到了一些洗衣服的技巧。这就是我学到的全部吗？不。我也学会了如何独立生活，毕竟你不能一辈子都依赖家人。
无论如何，做家务对我们来说非常重要，我们不仅可以帮助家人，还可以学习一些技能。
现在就为家人做点家务吧！
